Workflow

to final offer.

    Create student profile

    Students get clear information: role, eligibility, important dates and official form links.

  3. 03

    Start structured prep

    Students follow a simple weekly plan and use direct resource links for each topic.

  4. 04

    Track progress and status

    Dashboard shows preparation progress and application stage for each drive.

    Share outcomes and insights

    Placed students can share interview experiences to help the next batch prepare better.
