Privacy Policy

Effective date: March 23, 2026

1. Data We Collect

  • Basic profile details (name, email, phone, college, branch, year, CGPA).

2. How We Use Data

  • To provide personalized drive listings and preparation content.

  • To improve platform functionality and reliability.

3. Sharing of Data

We do not sell personal data. Relevant data may be visible to authorized college coordinators/admins for placement process management.

4. Third-Party Links

Nextern may redirect to company career portals and external prep websites. We are not responsible for privacy practices of those external sites.

5. Data Security

We use reasonable safeguards for data protection. Users should also keep account credentials secure.

6. Data Retention

We retain profile and activity data while your account remains active or as needed for placement record purposes.

7. Your Choices

  • You can update profile data from account pages.
  • You may request account deletion through your admin/coordinator channel.

8. Policy Updates

We may revise this policy as features evolve. Updated policy will be published on this page with a new effective date.
